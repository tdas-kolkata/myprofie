The Risk Return Scenario of Indian Equity Market

Overview
We have collected last 10 years daily price data for top 450 publicly traded indian companies consisted of the indices NIFTY 50 , NIFTY Next 50 , NIFTY Midcap 100 , NIFTY Smallcap 250 and performed the risk return analysis on the the one day rolling return . If we consider building our portfolio with one single stock then the graph below projects the map of annualised expected return and annualised portfolio variance (volatility) considering 250 business days in a year . If you follow Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT) the plot will help you to identify the efficient frontier of the entire indian equity market and Sharpe Ratio of individual securities in the equity market .
